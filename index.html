Đà Nẵng Night Tours

    Tour Information

    An amazing journey through the night sights and night life in Đà Nẵng city.

    • Five+ hours duration
    • Transportation throughout Đà Nẵng city
    • Visting engaging and popular tourist sites
    • Dining at a traditional seafood restaurant
    • One free drink ticket at a popular pub

    Trip Highlights and Itinerary

    The tour is structured as follows:

    • 5:15 PM: Start with a sunset view on the roof of a hi-rise apartment in Ngũ Hành Sơn district accompanied with a welcome drink!
    • 6 PM: Head over to Ẩm thực Xèo in Sơn Trà district for a seafood dinner
    • 7:30 PM: Walk to the Phạm Văn Đồng beach for a night stroll!
    • 9 PM: Go check out the fire-breathing DRAGON BRIDGE in Hải Châu district!
    • 9:45 PM: End the night at the popular Bamboo2 pub on the popular Bạch Đằng street
